Typeface and Type Foundry Resources

Since buying cool new typefaces can be expensive (and relying solely on the typefaces that come with your computer or software can be pretty limiting), here are a few resources about type foundries that offer student discounts/licensing or free trials

Download these files to use during in-class tech demo.

  • Alphabettes has pulled together a pretty thorough list of student discounts and the occasional free licensing for fonts that is definitely worth checking out.
  • Grilli Type is a great foundry that offers free trials of all their typefaces.
  • Rosetta is a foundry that allows you to download a typeface for free to test it or for student work. Just check the Test box on checkout page.
  • Fontstand is an app that will allow you to try typefaces for free for a limited time, or rent them by the month for much less than their full price. A fair number of great type foundries have their typefaces on Fontstand, so there’s a pretty good selection. They also give students €10 of credit to use towards renting typefaces.
  • The League of Movable Type offers a few high-quality, open-source (so free!) typefaces that you can download and use.
  • And, if a type foundry doesn’t explicitly say that they offer special student licensing options, it’s always worth shooting them an email to ask if they would consider licensing you one of their typefaces for free/cheap so that you can use it for a school project. Sometimes this works!
