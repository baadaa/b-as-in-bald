Editing Audio with Audacity

If you are interested in editing your own audio for the motion projects, consider getting started with Audacity.

Audacity is an open source software that you can use for audio editing. Download here. For a series of step-by-step tutorials for common tasks in Audacity, check this link.

If you are really ambitious, Adobe Audition will give you more robust and professional audio editing functionality but can be overwhelming.
