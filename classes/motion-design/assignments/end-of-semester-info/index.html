End of Semester Info

Important notes on end of semester deadlines and key final deliverables — requirements and guidelines.

Final Exam Period

Our class’s final exam period is Monday, May 8 from 3-6pm. As mentioned previously, you have the chance to revise any of your projects in the time between our final class and the final exam period. While you are not required to do this, but it will give you a chance to rework anything that you think you could improve upon.

Project Files Submission

Before or during the exam period, you’re required to turn in final, high-quality .MOV files for Projects 1, 2, and 4, as well as the GIFs you’ve produced for Project 3. 

  • If any of your projects have been revised, clearly mark them as such by putting “REVISED” somewhere in the file name.
  • Additionally, upload any revised projects to Vimeo and/or submit revised gifs to the Project 3 Tumblr.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.

Deadlines

  • If personally delivering files:
    • If you’re going to be physically present during the exam period to give me your final files, I’ll be in (or near) our usual classroom on May 8 at 3-6pm to collect everything.
  • If digitally transferring files:
    • If you’re planning on sending me a link to download your files—_Dropbox, Google Drive, etc_—you are not required to be physically present during our final exam period.
    • In this case, however, make sure to send me the files by Sunday, May 7 at 7pm. I will be downloading them that night, and I want to be sure there are no issues with downloading or with file corruption.
    • If you don’t digitally transfer your final deliverables by Sunday night I’ll expect to see you in person on Monday during 3-6pm.

We’re near the end! Good luck!
